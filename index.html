ABOUT

My background is concentrated in graphic design. I obtained my bachelors degree in fine arts with a concentration in graphic design from Wayne State University. My studies honed my creativity and gave me a skillset to visually communicate using typography, composition, and color. In my growth as a designer I understood I had to expand my skills because print is primarily consumed on the web. I enrolled in the Macomb Community College Interactive Web Media program where I was introduced to Html, Css, flash, Javascript, and several frameworks. MCC's cirriculum gave me general understanding of the web which narrowed the path I should focus on.

I wanted I a deeper understanding of web technologies and began to seriously pursue a career in web development so I enrolled in the Winter 2017 Grand Circus Front End Developer Bootcamp. The bootcamp ushered me into the Json framework and delved futher into bootstrap, Dom manipulation, and javascript . Collaborative work was also emphasized in result I hepled create two different applications using version control, Angular, and Jquery.

Problem solving is a universal skill that enhances your execution and expertise in any field of focus. I have learn this in my work experience and now as web developer.
